The Mojito: The perfect Summer cocktail, refreshing all year round


   Ingredients                         Measurements

Rum
50ml (2 shots)
White sugar
15g (2 teaspoons)
Soda Water
dash
Lime
½ a Lime
Fresh Mint Leaves
6 (+ extra for garnish)

Equipment

1x
Tall Glass
1x
Jigger
1x
Muddler
1x
Bar Spoon
1x
Knife
1x
Crushed ice

    Method

  1. Add the lime, cut into quarters to glass with the sugar and muddle (press together to release the lime juice)
  2. Add the mint leaves and press them down in the glass
  3. Add crushed ice to fill the cup ¾ full
  4. Pour in the rum and a dash of soda water
  5. Top up with crushed ice and garnish with fresh mint leaves(optional)

The Cosmopolitan: Bright, pink and classy


   Ingredients                        Measurements

Vodka
35ml
Triple Sec
10ml
Cranberry Juice
45ml
Lime Juice
10ml
Orange Peel
1 Twist

Equipment

1x
Martini Glass
1x
Jigger
1x
Cocktail Shaker
1x
Knife
1x
Crushed ice

    Method

  1. Fill the shaker with crushed ice
  2. Add the Vodka, Triple Sec, Cranberry and Lime juices to the shaker
  3. Shake until the surface of the shaker feels chilled.
  4. Strain into a martini glass
  5. Garnish with a twist of orange peel

The Martini: Shaken or Stirred?


   Ingredients                        Measurements

Gin
50ml (2 shots)
Dry Vermouth
5ml (1 teaspoon)
Olive
1 (optional garnish)

Equipment

1x
Martini Glass
1x
Jigger
1x
Blender
1x
Strainer
1x
Ice

    Method

  1. Fill a shaker with ice
  2. Add the Gin and Dry Vermouth to the shaker
  3. Stir, or shake the mixture vigorously until the surface of the cocktail shaker feels chilled
  4. Strain into a martini glass
  5. Garnish with an olive

The Old Fashioned: A true classic cocktail, with a history spanning almost 200 years


    Ingredients                         Measurements

Bourbon/ Rye Whiskey
50ml (2 shots)
Angostura Bitters
2 dashes
Sugar
1 cube
Cherry
1
Orange
Slice (optional garnish)

Equipment

1x
Short Glass
1x
Jigger
1x
Muddler
1x
Stirrer
1x
Knife
1x
Ice

    Method

  1. Place a sugar cube in a glass and add a dash of angostura bitters
  2. Muddle until dissolved (Press down)
  3. Fill the glass with ice
  4. Pour in the whiskey
  5. Garnish with a cherry and orange slice
