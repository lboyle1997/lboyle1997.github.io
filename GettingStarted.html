Getting Started? Here's what to do!




Mixology is an art that relies heavily on good craftmanship and decent tools, It can be a challenging and daunting task. Follow
these steps to begin your journey as a master of drinks!

STEP 1: JOIN THE SOCIETY

You can join the society via email, or calling, but we recommend you come along to a session, hosted every Tuesday at 7pm in the union's floor 5! This will give you a taste for the society and everything it has to offer as well as giving you a great chance to meet everyone! We're one of the most active Societies because every session feels like a night out! However we do also offer offcial nights out and bartending competitions - ALL INCLUDED IN THE Â£50 1 YEAR MEMBERSHIP!
There's never been a better time to sign up!




STEP 2: Get the gear

It's absolutely essential for every new bartender to be ready to serve any drink, and that means having the tools for the job. That means every new reruit should have mixers, strainers and the other essentials of bartending! As a society we stock the equipment that we think is the very best for beginners, which we then sell through our online store for prices cheaper than anywhere else!
Make sure you're ready to take on a new challenge!




STEP 3: Come along

As mentioned, we host our sessions on floor 5 every tuesday at 7pm. Every week a new category of drinks will be focused on, this allows for variation among seasoned professionals and allows the new recruits to learn new things. Tutors are always present during these sessions, so don't feel intimidated to ask for help! Here at the society we pride ourselves on the friendly and family-like atmosphere we create, it's an ideal way to meet new people and build strong friendships along the way!




STEP 4: HAVE FUN!

All that's left for you to do is sit back and relax. Attend the weekly sessions, learn new techniques, and make new friends,
and in no time at all you'll be a master mixer!
