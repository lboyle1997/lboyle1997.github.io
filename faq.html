We here at the Bartending Society pride ourselves in our ability to make world class drinks. From James Bond style Martinis to Scotlands own Bucky and Milk, we can help you learn how to develop your skills and find new friends. Only here at our society will you gain the skills necessary to show off to friends and make a scene at your next party.

Frequently Asked Questions

I've never done bartending before, where do I begin?

Don't worry if you have never attempted mixology before, we all have to have a first time. Head on over to our getting started page to begin your journey

I've finally got started but I need more recipes to try, any ideas?

Glad you asked! We have a huge collection of recipes for you to try, just head to our recipes page to satisfy your mixology needs

I don't drink, can I still get involved?

Yes you can! Every one of our lessons provides non-alcoholic alternatives to make sure we are all inclusive.

Right, I'm ready to go, where and when should I turn up?

Funny you asked, we hold lessons every Tuesday at 7pm so make sure to add it do your calender!

For any other queries you may have, please send them to dundeebartendingsociety@gmail.com
